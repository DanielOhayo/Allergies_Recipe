Ingredients:
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 4 cloves of minced garlic
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add the onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables have softened.
3. Stir in the basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper. Cook for another minute to release the flavors.
4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the liquid has reduced.
5. Once the vegetables are fully cooked, transfer the mixture to a blender or use an immersion blender to purée until smooth.
6. Taste and adjust the seasonings according to your preference.
7. The sauce is now ready to use! You can cook it further with sautéed vegetables or meat, serve it over pasta, or use it in any recipe calling for sauce without tomatoes.
