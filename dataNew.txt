Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon xanthan gum
- 1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk or any milk alternative
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 egg

Toppings:
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 cup shredded dairy-free mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup sliced black olives
- 1/4 cup sliced green bell pepper
- 1/4 cup sliced red onion

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (218°C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and xanthan gum.
3. Add the almond milk, olive oil, and egg. Stir until combined.
4. The dough will be sticky - place it onto a sheet of parchment paper.
5. Place another sheet of parchment paper on top of the dough and flatten out with your hands to form a circle. Remove the top sheet of parchment paper.
6. Bake the crust in the preheated oven for approximately 10 minutes or until golden brown.
7. Remove from the oven and add the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and garlic powder.
8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese, black olives, green bell pepper, and red onion over the pizza.
9. Return the pizza to the oven and bake for an additional 7-10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
10. Slice and enjoy!

I hope you enjoy this gluten-free and nut-free pizza recipe!
