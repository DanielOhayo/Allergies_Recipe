Ingredients:

- 1 pound pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- Any additional toppings of your choice (such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or peppers)

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees F.

2. Roll out your pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to the desired thickness.

3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.

5. Carefully transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.

7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza without nuts!
