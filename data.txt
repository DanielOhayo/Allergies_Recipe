Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp active dry yeast 
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
- Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, sausage, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast together. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy and bubbly.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
3. Pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil, and mix until a dough forms.
4. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with a towel. Allow to rise for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
6. Preheat your oven to 450Â°F.
7. Punch the dough down and stretch it out into a circle on a lightly floured surface.
8. Place the dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
9. Spread the pizza sauce on top of the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges.
10. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
11. Add your desired toppings.
12. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
13. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
