Of course! Here is a recipe for homemade pizza:

Ingredients:
- 1 pound pizza dough (can be homemade or store-bought)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- Toppings of your choice (such as pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, sliced onions, sliced bell peppers, etc.)
- Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface until it's your desired thickness.
3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
4. Spoon the tomato sauce onto the dough, spreading it evenly with the back of the spoon.
5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
6. Add your desired toppings.
7. Drizzle the pizza with olive oil.
8. Bake the pizza for about 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
